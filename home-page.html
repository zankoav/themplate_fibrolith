ROOM ACOUSTICS

Acoustic ceilings, wall absorbers, ceiling panels, or room dividers made of natural wood-wool reduce the reverberation times due to their sound-absorbing structure and ensure pleasant room acoustics. Here you can find out more about optimal solutions, creative practical examples, technology, and mounting as well as application areas with our acoustic board Fibro-Kustik.
