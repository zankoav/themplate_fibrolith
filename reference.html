REFERENCES

Here you will find a selection of objects that have been completed with our products.

Acoustic ceiling in a meditation center

A pleasant feeling of space, excellent acoustic properties and an appealing ceiling design - These are important requirements for relaxing and meditating. We call that “Spirit of Wood”.

Fibro-Kustik's wood-wool acoustic boards from the "Spirit of Wood" edition were designed with the "golden ray of sunlight" in super fine wood-wool in the format of 1200 x 600 mm with a small bevel.

Healing awareness trainer Mrs. Judith Pick speaking about the acoustic situation before and after the acoustic ceiling renovation in her practice for awareness expansion: “Thanks to the ceiling boards “Spirit of Wood” the acoustics have improved considerably in my seminar facility. For my seminars, the new, soothing acoustics without noise is very encouraging. At the same time the boards with their beautiful optical design are a real eye-catcher. "
