PRODUCTS

Wood wool boards from Fibrolith are ideal for room acoustics and ball impact resistance coverings of walls and ceilings as well as a plaster base and for the insulation in the interior and exterior because of their sound-absorbing effect, their strength, fire safety and material structure.

In addition, the boards offer unimagined design possibilities for interior design. Creative planners use the combination of excellent acoustics, characteristic appearance and natural material as highlights on walls, ceilings, partitions, beams, columns or girders.

Decorative room acoustics

Acoustics is a central aspect for a pleasant sense of space. If this is not true and you hear an echo after you speak, you quickly feel uncomfortable. The causes of this unpleasant effect are generally hard and smooth surfaces that are not sufficiently sound absorbent. The acoustic boards Fibro-Kustik offer ideal solutions for improving room acoustics due to its sound-absorbing wood wool structure.
