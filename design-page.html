UNEXPECTED DESIGN POSSIBILITIES

With light-weight boards of Fibrolith wood wool

In addition to sustainable building material with excellent physical properties (acoustics, fire protection, thermal insulation), the light-weight boards of Fibrolith wood wool offers unimagined design possibilities for interior design.

Especially in rooms where pleasant acoustics plays an important role, such as in bars, restaurants, sports facilities, shops, public buildings, creative designers combine the acoustic benefits with the optical effect of the board structure. The boards can be used for more than just ceiling linings, but also as a partial highlight on walls, partitions, beams, supports, or carriers.

Whether in natural, white or colored, with a fine or extra fine structure Fibrolith wood-wool light-weight boards with their characteristic appearance meet the architectural spirit of the times, where purist and natural materials are in high demand.
